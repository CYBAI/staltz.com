Hi, I'm a developer and designer, specialized in web user interfaces and Android development.

ABOUT ME

I come from Brazil, but have no interest in football and many other things that identify them. I'm rare. Now I live in Finland, where it's nice and cold.

I work as Software Engineer at Futurice. I'm the founder of Iroquote, a platform for online discussion societies.

I am comfortable developing both backend and frontend sides of software, but my focus is on highly interactive user interfaces. My passion is to make great ideas become reality, with obsessive attention to detail.

You might have read an introduction to Rx that I wrote. Currently, I'm very enthusiastic about Rx and user interfaces.

FIND ME

EXTRAS

I'm MSc in Computer Science, and for my Master's Thesis I developed HAcid, a Java client library that enables ACID transactions on HBase. In Brazil I graduated BSc in Computational Mathematics at UFMG, where I got to do research on Computational Fluid Dynamics and also Operations Research.

Occassionally I write on Medium about entrepreneurship and software development.

Sometimes I make games. With my friend Glauber Kotaki I've made some under the label ZeroDuo, and by myself I've made RGBlend, a color puzzle game. I've also had an important role in a community of brazilian game designers named Gamebrain.

I'm an acoustic guitarrist and a composer. I listen to different genres, but mostly instrumental music. Check out my acoustic songs recorded on video. In 2004-2006 I recorded a progressive alternative rock/metal album called O vazio tem um nome. You can find other compositions at Soundcloud.

MY NAME

Staltz is just a nickname I use on the web since 2004. My real name is André Medeiros, but I try to avoid it because it's disappointingly unoriginal.
