ABOUT ME

I come from Brazil, but have no interest in football and many other things that identify them. I'm rare. Now I live in Finland, where it's nice and cold.

I work as User Interface Engineer at Futurice, building Android apps and web apps. In the past I have been an entrepreneur and full stack developer at my startup Iroquote, a platform for online discussion forums.

I have done programming in all kinds of fields, but I have discovered that my passion and focus is with highly interactive user interfaces. I want to make great ideas become reality, with obsessive attention to detail.

You might have read an introduction to Rx that I wrote. Currently, I'm very enthusiastic about Rx. I have built RxMarbles, designed ReactiveX.io, and my current mission is to redefine how we structure and understand user interfaces with the reactive framework Cycle.js.

EXTRAS

I'm MSc in Computer Science, and for my Master's Thesis I developed HAcid, a Java client library that enables ACID transactions on HBase. In Brazil I graduated BSc in Computational Mathematics at UFMG, where I got to do research on Computational Fluid Dynamics and also Operations Research.

Occassionally I write on Medium about entrepreneurship and software development.

Sometimes I make games. With my friend Glauber Kotaki I've made some games such as Cold Tension, and by myself I've made RGBlend, a color puzzle game. I've also had an important role in a community of brazilian game designers named Gamebrain.

I'm an acoustic guitarrist and a composer. I listen to different genres, but mostly instrumental music. Check out my acoustic songs recorded on video. In 2004-2006 I recorded a progressive alternative rock/metal album called O vazio tem um nome. You can find other compositions at Soundcloud.
